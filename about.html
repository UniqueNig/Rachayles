About Us

My passion for design is innate. My purchase of a 120-year-old large Victorian house helped actualize it.

After a gut renovation with a 1500 square foot 3-story extension (which i conceived, planned and supervised), i decided to pursue my lifelong dream of a career in interior design (following many years in the acccounting field). My mathematical aptitude, complements the space planning component of design.

I love taking a space, envisioning all its possibilities and maximizing its potential. My impeccable attention to detail and intuitive understanding of my clients and their needs, has served me well. i have completed numerous projects and brought them to fruition seamlessly and with confidence. many more projects are currently in progress.
